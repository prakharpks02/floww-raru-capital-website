IIBX Broking

Transforming Opportunities in Billion Markets

Raru Capital IFSC Pvt Ltd recently became a Trading Member of the International Bullion Exchange (IIBX), marking a significant expansion of its capabilities in the bullion market. This membership allows Raru Capital to trade bullion not only on its proprietary account but also on behalf of its clients, offering a comprehensive suite of services within the GIFT City financial ecosystem. By gaining access to IIBX, Raru Capital can now participate in the transparent and efficient trading of precious metals, further enhancing its position in the international bullion market. This development underscores our commitment to providing innovative and diversified financial solutions to our clients.
